Marina Creek Apartments

Marina Creek Apartments offers spacious one and two bedroom apartments. All units include a large family room and a cozy kitchen. New, double-pane windows have recently been installed and each unit includes air conditioning and a ceiling fan. The kitchens have had tile floors installed and there is an on-site laundry facility.

The Marina Creek Apartments complex is located in Antioch, within a short walking distance of neighborhood parks and the county fairgrounds. The Marina is also just a five minute drive or a 20 minute walk from the property.

Apartments available to rent now! Join us at Marina Creek Apartments and enjoy on-site amenities and local conveniences.
